Giving Back

Most people want to contribute; they just need an easier way of doing so. We have teamed up with some of the best charities in the world, and made supporting their causes as easy as possible.

Buying a costume on Chrysalis will save you money. All we ask is that a small fraction of your savings contributes to something greater. When you donate, we donate.

Chrysalis also cycles in fundraisers in response to humanitarian crises. We host a few days a year where 100% of our profits go directly to aid for disaster relief.
