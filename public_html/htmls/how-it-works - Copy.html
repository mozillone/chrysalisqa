You have two options...

If you have 3 or more costumes to sell you can request a costume cleanout kit. We send you a large bag with a prepaid shipping label. You simply place your old costumes in the bag and bring it to the post office; we pay you for the costumes we are able to accept. We offer cash or Chrysalis Store Credit for your next costume purchase.

If you have less than 3 costumes, or something so large it cannot fit into our cleanout bags, you can sell it yourself by uploading it onto Chrysalis. This is a great option for large custom made Comic Con costumes, mascot costumes, suits of armor, and unique homemade items. This option is a little more time intensive but it offers the best payout.

Additional Information

01. Selling Requirements

For a costume to be sold on Chrysalis it needs to be in excellent condition. We donâ€™t expect perfection, very slight wear is acceptable, but the costume must be complete, with no defects or flaws that could compromise utility for the next owner.

02. Cleanliness Requirements

Costumes sold directly to other users need to be clean and ready to be worn. Costumes sent to Chrysalis are inspected and cleaned prior to resale. However, we do not accept excessively dirty costumes with obvious stains or odors.

03. Request a Bag

In order for us to have a chance to offer you a payout you need to have at least three costumes to request a costume cleanout kit. These costumes need to pass our cleanliness and selling requirements.

04. Upload a Costume - Photo Guide

The easiest way to upload photos is to use the Chrysalis app. You can also upload the photos on our site. We recommend taking a few photos showing the entire costume as well as a detail photo showing any spots with wear or minor flaws, or any accessories. As a general rule, do not photograph anything that is not included with the costume.

05. Chrysalis' Referral Program

If you believe in our vision help us make it a reality. Get your friends and family members to send us a Costume Cleanout Kit and both of you get $5 worth of Chrysalis Store Credit.

06. How Does Chrysalis Profit?

We charge a 3% transaction fee for every user uploaded costume. We also make a small margin on costumes we resell.
